1. Magic

Magic is begin of superblock or descriptor of block's pull.

There are two magic:

  - 'VvChFsSb' - SuperBlock
  - 'VvChFsBp' - Blocks Pull

Magic can be found in first 64 megabytes of disk with offsets:
0, 1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, 64, 128, 256, 512, 1024, 2048, ... HW blocks

driver will loop over powers of 2 offsets until 64 MB exceeded.

Magics are optional. Main purpose for magics - automated finding
of SB on media.

2. Mounting

Supper block can be found on storage or given in mount options.

If superblock is given in mount options then there are no need
in any magics on media. Also no media scanning required.
